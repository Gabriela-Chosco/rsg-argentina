Day 2 - Simposium Program

11:00-11:50

Dr. Cesar Luis Avila

Markov state models in protein ligand-binding analysis: finding the needle in a haystack

The increase in computational power has led to larger simulation times allowing us to explore more interesting phenomenas. Nevertheless, the enormous size of the associated datasets pose new challenges on the analysis techniques to derive meaningful information. In this talk we will discuss how Markov State Models provide a mean to analyze binding kinetics data from multiple simulations.
